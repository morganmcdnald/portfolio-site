My Story So Far

Click along the timeline to read about my academic journey.

Finished Secondary School, Leaving with 6 Highers and 1 Advanced Higher

In 2015 I began my first HND, starting with Cyber Security before settling on Computer Science.

In June 2017 I finished up my HND where I left with an A in both Graded Units.

The following August I started my BSc Computing Science at the University of Glasgow, fast tracking due to my HND.

2 years later I graduated university with a BSc in Computing Science.

That August I began a PGDE in Secondary Education at the University of Glasgow with views to become a Computing teacher.

After 4 months I left the PGDE course in January 2020 after realising I was going down the wrong route. Used the following months to work and learn web development skills.

In August 2020 I began my HND Web Engineering in order to learn more about web design and development.

I am scheduled to finish my first year of my HND in June 2021. Up next is year 2 or full time employment.

After completing this year at college, my aim is to find a full time job in Front-End Development or Design.
